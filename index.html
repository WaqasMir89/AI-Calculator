FAQ

What is a token?

A token is a unit of text used by AI models. It can be a word, part of a word, or even punctuation.

How much does AI cost per token?

Costs vary by model. Some models charge per million tokens for both input and output.

How can I reduce token costs?

Use smaller models, shorten prompts, and limit output length.
